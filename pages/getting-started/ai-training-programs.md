# AI Training Programs

Comprehensive AI training resources to build capability across MYOB teams, from foundational knowledge to advanced implementation skills.

## Overview

AI literacy and practical skills are essential for modern software development. This page provides curated training resources across different learning styles and expertise levels, including internal MYOB programs, external courses, certifications, and hands-on learning opportunities.

## MYOB Internal Programs

### Structured Enablement Programs
- **AI Workshops**: Hands-on sessions covering prompt engineering, AI-assisted code review, testing strategies, and security practices
- **Role-Based Certifications**: Tailored learning paths for developers, testers, product managers, and architects (refreshed quarterly)
- **AI Office Hours**: Weekly clinics providing support for teams adopting AI tools and practices
- **Learning Pathways**: Progressive skill development from beginner to practitioner to advanced levels

### MYOB AI Initiatives
- **AI in MYOB Platform Integration**: Training on MYOB's AI-powered features and capabilities
- **AI-Powered Compliance Engine**: Workshops on leveraging Silverfin's AI workpaper and financial reporting platform
- **Enterprise AI Strategy**: Sessions on scaling AI adoption across business units

## Getting Started

### Immediate Actions
1. **Access LinkedIn Learning**: Use your MYOB corporate account
2. **Start with Fundamentals**: Choose Azure AI-900 or AWS ML Foundations
3. **Join MYOB AI Office Hours**: Weekly sessions every [Day/Time - Contact Architecture Team]
4. **Subscribe to Key Channels**: 3Blue1Brown, Two Minute Papers for ongoing learning

### Assessment and Planning
- **Skills Assessment**: Complete the AI readiness assessment (available in MYOB Learning Portal)
- **Individual Learning Plan**: Work with your manager to create a personalized AI learning roadmap
- **Team Training**: Contact the Architecture team for team-specific AI enablement programs

## Measuring Success

Track your AI learning progress and impact:
- **Completion Metrics**: Courses completed, certifications earned
- **Application Metrics**: AI tools adopted, productivity improvements
- **Quality Metrics**: Code quality, testing coverage, security compliance
- **Team Metrics**: Knowledge sharing, peer mentoring, workshop participation

For detailed measurement frameworks, see [Continuous Improvement](../feature-development/continuous-improvement.md).
