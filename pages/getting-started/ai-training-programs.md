# AI Training Programs

---

Comprehensive AI training resources to build capability across MYOB teams, from foundational knowledge to advanced implementation skills.

## Overview

AI literacy and practical skills are essential for modern software development. This page provides curated training resources across different learning styles and expertise levels, including internal MYOB programs, external courses, certifications, and hands-on learning opportunities.

## MYOB Internal Programs

### Structured Enablement Programs
- **AI Workshops**: Hands-on sessions covering prompt engineering, AI-assisted code review, testing strategies, and security practices
- **Role-Based Certifications**: Tailored learning paths for developers, testers, product managers, and architects (refreshed quarterly)
- **AI Office Hours**: Weekly clinics providing support for teams adopting AI tools and practices
- **Learning Pathways**: Progressive skill development from beginner to practitioner to advanced levels

## Learning Paths by Role

### Developers
1. **Foundation**: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (LinkedIn Learning)
2. **AI Development Tools**: Cole Medin (Cursor), Rob Shocks (GitHub Copilot), AI Labs (latest tools)
3. **Technical Skills**: AWS/Azure AI certifications + Prompt Engineering course
4. **Practical Application**: GPT-4 Foundations + Matthew Berman (model reviews)
5. **Advanced**: 3Blue1Brown (theory), IndyDevDan (best practices), Fireship (modern tools)

### Product Managers
1. **Business Context**: Generative AI for Business Leaders (LinkedIn Learning)
2. **Strategy**: Scaling Generative AI (LinkedIn Learning)
3. **Tool Understanding**: AI Jason (business applications), The AI Advantage (productivity)
4. **Industry Insights**: Matthew Berman (AI news), AI Business YouTube channel
5. **Implementation**: Glean AI training

### Testers/QA
1. **AI Testing Fundamentals**: MYOB internal workshops
2. **Automated Testing**: Tech with Tim, IndyDevDan (best practices)
3. **AI Quality Assurance**: Azure AI-102 certification
4. **Tool Reviews**: All About AI, AI Labs (testing new tools)
5. **Security Testing**: AWS AI security workshops

### Architects
1. **Enterprise AI Strategy**: Responsible AI course (LinkedIn Learning)
2. **Technical Architecture**: AWS Machine Learning Specialty
3. **Research and Trends**: Two Minute Papers, Yannic Kilcher, Machine Learning Street Talk
4. **Industry Analysis**: Lex Fridman interviews, AI Coffee Break
5. **Implementation Patterns**: MYOB internal advanced workshops

## Getting Started

### Immediate Actions
1. **Access LinkedIn Learning**: Use your MYOB corporate account
2. **Start with Fundamentals**: Choose Azure AI-900 or Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
3. **Subscribe to Top AI Channels**: Cole Medin (Cursor), Matthew Berman (AI news), AI Labs (latest tools)
4. **Join MYOB AI Office Hours**: Weekly sessions every [Day/Time - Contact Architecture Team]
5. **Follow Development Tools**: Rob Shocks (GitHub Copilot), IndyDevDan (best practices)

### Assessment and Planning
- **Skills Assessment**: Complete the AI readiness assessment (available in MYOB Learning Portal)
- **Individual Learning Plan**: Work with your manager to create a personalized AI learning roadmap
- **Team Training**: Contact the Architecture team for team-specific AI enablement programs

## Measuring Success

Track your AI learning progress and impact:
- **Completion Metrics**: Courses completed, certifications earned
- **Application Metrics**: AI tools adopted, productivity improvements
- **Quality Metrics**: Code quality, testing coverage, security compliance
- **Team Metrics**: Knowledge sharing, peer mentoring, workshop participation

For detailed measurement frameworks, see [Continuous Improvement](../feature-development/continuous-improvement.md).
